## üì± View Mobile View

### In Browser (Recommended for Development)

1. **Start the server**: `./run.sh` or `npm run dev`
2. **Open Chrome/Edge** and go to: http://localhost:3000
3. **Open DevTools**:
   - Mac: `Cmd + Option + I`
   - Windows/Linux: `F12`
4. **Toggle Device Toolbar**:
   - Mac: `Cmd + Shift + M`
   - Windows/Linux: `Ctrl + Shift + M`
5. **Select Device**: Choose "iPhone 14 Pro" or "iPhone SE" from dropdown

### On Your Mobile Device

1. **Start with network access**: `npm run dev:network`
2. **Find your IP**: Your IP is **10.0.0.38** (may change)
3. **On your phone**: Open http://10.0.0.38:3000
4. **Make sure**: Phone and computer are on same WiFi network
